Why skytale.it

Andrea Gigante


I have always had a passion for security and encryption.
As a child, I enjoyed passing notes between friends using a form of steganography we invented. Later, with my first computer, I began to understand the foundational concepts of encryption.

A scytale is a tool used to perform a transposition cipher, it consisting of a cylinder with a strip of parchment wound around it on which is written a message. The ancient Greeks, and the Spartans in particular, are said to have used this cipher to communicate during military campaigns Wikipedia Scytale

.it is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Italy, my country of origin and at the same time IT is as well the acronym for Information technology, a field I have grown up with, studied and have been working for years.

For me, Skytale symbolizes clarity, precision, and secure communication—values I strive to embody in my professional journey.
